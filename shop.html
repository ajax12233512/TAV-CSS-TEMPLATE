Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I get an offer to sell my car?

There are two easy ways to get an offer. You can either fill out our online form at texasautovalue.com, or call us directly at (469) 547-1642

How much will you pay for my vehicle?

We assess each vehicle individually, factoring in its mileage, history, and current condition so that we can give you the highest value for your car possible.

How long will the offer last?

Our offer lasts for 3 days.

How long will it take to get my offer?

Same day up to 24 hours for our representative to contact you.

What type of vehicles do you buy?

We only purchase cars that have never been in any accidents. No Rebuilt, Lemmon, Salvage, or anything else other than a clean Carfax vehicle.

I want to sell you my vehicle. When can I come?

We will schedule appointments the same day or within 24 hours of agreement.

What if i've lost my title?

Depending on your state we will typically meet at the DMV for a new one together.

What should I bring to the bank?

Bring the title or payoff information for your car, as well as your valid, current registration and driver’s license. If you still owe money on the car, please bring your lien account number. We will bring Bill of Sale and any other documents needed to sale your car. Also bring all copies of the car’s keys, key fobs, or remote entry fobs.

How will you pay me?

We pay for all vehicles in full before we leave the bank. Payment can be made in cash, wire, cashiers check, or company check your choice.

Where do I sign the title?

1. Sign your name on the SIGNATURE OF OWNER line on the front of title.

2. On the Back, go to the ASSIGNMENT OF TITLE box. There you will:
- Indicate Odometer reading
- Put down the date of sale
- Print your name (on the line that says, well, PRINT YOUR NAME (seller)
- Sign your name again. Guess where? On the line labeled SIGNATURE OF SELLER, of course.

If multiple owners are listed on the front of the title, all of them sign and print their names as it SHOWS on the title.
