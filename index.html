About Us

The only way we will do the deal is to check out your vehicle FACE TO FACE. We will pay in CASH, CASHIERS CHECK, or WIRE – your money, your way. Everything will get notarized at the bank to ensure everything is done correctly.

Making it Simple

Tell us about your vehicle, its history, and current condition. We’ll calculate an instant, haggle-free offer. Choose a convenient date and time to meet you at – your bank – or any bank, if you have title-in-hand. We’ll take a look at the vehicle to make sure everything is good to go, then complete the paperwork with a bank representative. That’s it … and it only takes up to 30 minutes.
